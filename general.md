> <!here>Sorry to interrupt but this is an important message for community-bioc.  The Slack Pro support for this community has cost the Bioconductor Foundation $10,000.00 or more for each of the past few years.  This cannot be sustained and we are planning to terminate the Pro plan some time in April.  Slack free will remain available, but access to old messages will be lost.
